Timely:

Timely is a data visualization tool that inputs and outputs chain of custody and evidence form information in three ways:

Form

    -Features
  • Highlighted form fields to increase readability
  • Hover tooltips on field forms to increase ease of use
  • Simple and straight forward buttons
    • Load: to load data to table
    • Populate: to populate the timeline
    • Save: to download the table data in a .csv Excel document
    • Reset: to clear the table and timeline data

Table

    -Features
  • Easy to read format that can be referenced via the timeline by case number
  • Scroll function for inputs
  • Offline capability with peresistent storage

Timeline

    -Features
  • Can handle multiple items
  • Zoom feature: Max zoom in shows 4 hour intervals of one day | Max zoom out shows 3 months at a time
  • Tooltip on hover
  • Offline capability

Purpose: to allow forensic investigators to utilize the need for careful documentaiton that builds the integrity of their case in a visual way that helps them both reference multiple cases and details quickly to assist in court proceedings.
